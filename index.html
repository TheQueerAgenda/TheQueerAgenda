The Agenda

Welcome to The Queer Agenda! This is a queering of Seattle and its politics. The Queer agenda is more than equality. The Queer Agenda is justice. It is more than a solution, it is a commitment. The Queer Agenda is about committing to EACH OTHER! Join us!

#BlackLivesMatter is The Queer Agenda. Stop the displacement of black and brown people from our city center.

Trans Liberation is The Queer Agenda. Stopping I-1552 is imperative because it will legalize discrimination if it passes. Washington won’t discriminate. Seattle won’t discriminate. The Queer Agenda is pro-trans. The Queer Agenda Declines to Sign.

The Queer Agenda is about the issues where our identities intersect, like homelessness and public safety. The City is playing a game of wack-a-mole to resolve homelessness, and The Queer Agenda is No-Sweeps. We demand an institutional shift in our response to homelessness.

The Queer Agenda knows that housing, healthcare, and safety are a human right. The Queer Agenda demands safe spaces. Sidewalks and stickers are bullshit; they don’t solve hate-violence. The Queer Agenda needs an LGBTQIA community center and real safe spaces.

The Queer Agenda is you, and you are The Queer Agenda. Share your contact information and we'll reach out to get your input. And, PLEASE, register to vote by July 3. THIS IS MAJORLY IMPORTANT! Voting is the first and easiest way to get started on a path to transformation: THE QUEER AGENDA.
