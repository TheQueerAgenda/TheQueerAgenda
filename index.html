The Agenda

Welcome to The Queer Agenda. This is our queering of Seattle, and its politics. We want more than equality. We want justice. But justice is not a solution, it is a commitment. The Queer Agenda is about committing to EACH OTHER! Please join us!

Black Lives Matter is The Queer Agenda. Stop the displacement of black people from our city center.

The Queer Agenda is pro-trans. Stop I-1552 which would repeal anti-discrimination policies and codify anti-trans hate into Washington law.

The Queer Agenda is about the issues where our identities intersect, like homelessness and public safety. Since the city declared a state of emergency because of the homelessness crisis, we have seen it do little besides play a game of wack-a-mole, as homeless campers are swept from one place to the next. We demand institutional shifts in our collective response to homelessness, starting with an end to the sweeps. The Queer Agenda knows that housing, healthcare, and safety are a human right. The Queer Agenda demands safe spaces. Sidewalks and stickers aren't enough. It's going to take more from the city. You care about our community? Prove it. We want a community center where we can be our full, visible selves.

We look forward to joining forces with you. Share your contact information and we'll reach out. And, PLEASE, register to vote here by July 3. That is the first and easiest way to get started on a path to transformation.
